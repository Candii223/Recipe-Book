Brownies


Ingredients:

  • 3 Eggs
  • 3 TBSP Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 pinch Salt
  • 1 TBSP Vanilla Extract
  • 3/4 cup White Sugar

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Grease a baking pan.
  3. Beat eggs and add oil, cocoa powder, vanilla, sugar, and salt to make the batter.
  4. Bake until the top is dry, and the edges pull away from the baking pan.
