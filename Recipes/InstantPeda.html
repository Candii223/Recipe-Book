Instant Peda


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup roated gram
  • 1/2 cup ghee
  • 1/3 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom

Directions:

  1. Blend the roasted gram and set aside.
  2. Take the powdered sugar and ghee and mix it.
  3. Add cardamom powder to the ghee and sugar.
  4. Add the powdered gram and add it to the sugar mix.
  5. Kneed the dough and make small balls.

Enjoy!
