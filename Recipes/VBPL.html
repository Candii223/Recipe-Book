Very Berry Peach Lemonade


Ingredients:

  • 6 Large Strawberries
  • 1/2 cup Blackberries
  • 5 Peaches Peeled and Chopped
  • 1/4 cup Honey
  • 6 Lemons Juiced

Directions:

  1. Blend strawberries, blackberries, peaches, and honey together. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the fruit puree to remove pulp and seeds.
  3. Add 6 cups of water to the lemon juice.
  4. Add the fruit puree to the lemon juice and water.
  5. Pour over ice and enjoy!
